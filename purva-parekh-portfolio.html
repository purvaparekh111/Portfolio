03 · Flagship Build

The AI project I'm proudest of.

Oct 2025 – Feb 2026

Women Safety App

An Android safety app with a basic AI assistant for emergency guidance — built using RAG concepts to pull accurate safety info and LangChain to manage context, on top of shake-detection SOS alerts and live GPS tracking.

  • OpenAI-powered assistant for emergency actions & helplines
  • RAG retrieval over structured safety resources to cut hallucination
  • FAISS / ChromaDB vector search for contacts & help content
  • Shake-detection → auto SMS with live GPS to saved contacts
  • Google Maps integration for nearby police stations & navigation
  • SOS calling, fake incoming call, real-time location sharing
